Pressure Model

Pressure is continuous, not stepped — zones just define what gear is needed. The formula: pressure_atm = 1.0 + (depth_m / 10.0). Each piece of dive gear has a maxPressure rating. When ambient pressure exceeds gear rating, damage ticks begin — scaling linearly with the excess. No gear at all means damage starts at ~15m (1.5 atm, with a small grace buffer for the Sunlit zone).

Ascent sickness: rising faster than 10 m/s from below 30m triggers a disorientation debuff (camera wobble, blurred vision) lasting 5–10 seconds. This discourages rocket-ascents and adds tactical depth — you must plan your return.

Visibility & Rendering

Fog distance decreases with depth: fogEnd = maxVisibility * (1.0 - depth/maxDepth)². Color shift: water absorbs red first → scene tints blue-green in twilight, blue-black in midnight, pure black in abyss. Light sources (player lamp, bioluminescent creatures) punch through fog with a pointLightRadius that also decreases with depth due to particulate scattering. All configured via depth_zones.json — no hardcoded depth thresholds.
