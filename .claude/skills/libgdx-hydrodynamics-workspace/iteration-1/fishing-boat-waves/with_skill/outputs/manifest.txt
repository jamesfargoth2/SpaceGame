FISHING BOAT WATER PHYSICS - FILE MANIFEST
============================================

Components (ECS data)
---------------------
water/WaveParams.java                        - Gerstner wave parameters (amplitude, wavelength, speed, steepness, direction)
water/BuoyancySamplePoint.java               - Hull surface patch for multi-point buoyancy sampling (offset, normal, area)
water/WaterBodyComponent.java                - Defines a body of water with fluid properties and wave definitions
water/HullComponent.java                     - Hull characteristics for floating vessels (mass, drag coefficients, dimensions, sample points)
water/WakeComponent.java                     - Kelvin wake trail data for rendering (Froude number, intensity, trail positions)
water/components/BoatInputComponent.java     - Player input state for boat piloting (throttle, steering), normalised [-1,1]
water/components/BoatMotorComponent.java     - Motor and rudder properties (thrust, torque, response rates, speed-dependent rudder)

Systems (ECS logic)
-------------------
water/systems/WaveSystem.java                - Evaluates Gerstner wave surface height/normal/flow at galaxy-space coords; service system queried by buoyancy and drag
water/systems/BuoyancySystem.java            - Multi-point hull buoyancy: hydrostatic pressure per sample point with hull-normal forces producing emergent roll/pitch restoring torques
water/systems/HydrodynamicDragSystem.java    - Axis-decomposed drag (skin friction, form drag, wave-making drag) plus quadratic angular damping for heavy turning feel
water/systems/BoatMotorSystem.java           - Translates player input to thrust/rudder forces; exponential smoothing for analog feel; speed-dependent rudder effectiveness
water/systems/WakeTrailSystem.java           - Emits stern positions into wake trail for rendering; fades trail when vessel slows
water/systems/VesselBuoyancySystem.java      - Alternative buoyancy system using WaveQuery utility (complementary to BuoyancySystem)

Events
------
water/events/VesselEnteredWaterEvent.java    - Published when a vessel's first sample point submerges (for audio/VFX splash)
water/events/VesselExitedWaterEvent.java     - Published when a vessel's last sample point clears the surface
water/events/CapsizeEvent.java               - Published when vessel roll exceeds 60 degrees while in water

Utilities
---------
water/WaveQuery.java                         - Stateless utility for Gerstner wave height and normal evaluation (used by buoyancy)

Factory
-------
water/FishingBoatFactory.java                - Assembles a fishing boat entity with 16 hull sample points, motor, wake, and Bullet rigid body

SYSTEM PRIORITIES (execution order)
------------------------------------
 1  WaveSystem          - evaluate wave surface (runs first)
 5  BuoyancySystem      - apply buoyancy forces per hull sample point
11  HydrodynamicDragSystem - apply viscous, form, and wave-making drag + angular damping
12  BoatMotorSystem     - apply thrust and rudder forces from player input
13  WakeTrailSystem     - update wake trail for rendering

PHYSICS SUMMARY
---------------
- Buoyancy: 16-point hull sampling with hydrostatic pressure forces along hull normals
- Drag: axis-decomposed (forward skin friction, lateral form drag, wave-making hump at Froude ~0.45)
- Steering: speed-dependent rudder effectiveness (10% at rest, 100% at 3+ m/s)
- Input smoothing: exponential approach for weighty analog feel
- All forces via btRigidBody.applyCentralForce/applyTorque (never bypass Bullet)
- Wave phase in galaxy-space doubles for floating-origin continuity
- Velocity relative to water current (drag against fluid, not ground)
