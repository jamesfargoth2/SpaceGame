PHYSICS MODEL:
  - Buoyancy: F_up = rho * g * V_submerged; F_down = m_effective * g; net = F_up - F_down
  - Effective mass = dry_mass + ballast_water_mass + flood_water_mass
  - Depth control: PID(targetDepth - currentDepth) -> ballast fill fraction
  - Drag: F_drag = -0.5 * rho * |v|^2 * Cd * A per axis (body-local frame)
  - Hull damage: quadratic with crush depth overshoot; breaches at integrity thresholds
  - Flooding: pressure-proportional ingress; water spreads through degrading bulkheads
  - Stability: metacentric height restoring torques + flood-induced roll/pitch asymmetry
  - Waves: sum-of-sines with exponential depth attenuation (30m half-life)
